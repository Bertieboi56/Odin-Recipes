BurgerHelper!

This homemade "hamburger" is far better than mcdonalds!, with fresh spices and a hint of tomato, it's beefy, cheesey, savory and has all the makings of a weeknight family favourite

  • 1 pound 85% lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup diced yellow onion
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 6 ounces Cheddar cheese, shredded (about 1 1/2 cups)
